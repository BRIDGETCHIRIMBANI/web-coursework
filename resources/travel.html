Enjoy Wonderful Experiences in Zimbabwe

Victoria Falls

Victoria Falls is a spectacular sight of awe-inspiring beauty and grandeur on the Zambezi River.

Its indigenous name is Mosi-oa-Tunya and its associated with eight steep sided gorges which have been formed through changing waterfall positions.

Great Zimbabwe

Great Zimbabwe was a medieval African city known for its large circular stone wall and tower.

It was part of a wealthy African trading empire that controlled much of the East African coast from the 11th to the 15th centuries C.E.

Kariba Dam

The Kariba Dam is a double curvature concrete arch dam in the Kariba Gorge of the Zambezi river basin between Zambia and Zimbabwe.

It is the sole provider of hydro-electric power in Zimbabwe and provides kapenta and fish for the nation.

Nyanga Mountains

Mount Nyangani is the highest mountain in Zimbabwe at 2,592 m. The mountain is located within Nyanga National Park in Nyanga District.

There is a long-held belief by the Manyika people living here that the mountain is a sacred highland area.
